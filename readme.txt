== Description ==
[Homepage](https://devpups.com/) | [Documentation](http://docs.devpups.com/) | [Premium Version](https://devpups.com/skyepress/)

**Auto post to your Social Media profiles**

Auto post to Facebook and Twitter when publishing your articles. Set custom messages for each social media profile in order to maximize your return from each social network.

**Schedule posts to be shared on a later date**

Create custom schedules that will look into your already published posts to re-share them on your social media profiles. 

= Main Features =
* **Auto Post to Facebook** when publishing a new article
* **Auto Post to Twitter** when publishing a new article
* **Create custom schedules** that will auto post to Facebook and Twitter your existing posts on the dates and times you specify
* **Add custom messages** to be shared on social media for each Facebook and Twitter account


= Premium Features =
* **Post to LinkedIn** - Connect your LinkedIn profile or page and share your posts to LinkedIn
* **Connect multiple account** - Connect up to 50 social media profiles and share your posts on all of them
* **Unlimited custom schedules** - Create unlimited custom schedules to share existing posts to your connected social profiles
* **Share custom post types** - Share posts, pages and any custom post type. Filter schedules by post types and taxonomies
* **Share image posts** - Share the featured image of the post alongside a custom message

== Installation ==

1. Upload the skyepress folder to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Activate each location where you wish the buttons to appear
